Information

I offer you a professional work ethic and quality for reasonable rates

Contact me so we can discuss how I can help you with your project. My rates are reasonable and tailored to each individual project, so when requesting a quote, please include detailed information about the project you feel comfortable sharing. On video games, gameplay video is always the best choice, but concept art, references or a design document is also helpful in determining the musical style required.

I can compose a short demo for review according to your requirements.
